#Retail Insights Dashboard | Power BI
## Project Overview

The Retail Insights Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to help retail businesses analyze store performance, evaluate promotional effectiveness, and monitor product/category performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales trends, promotional impact, and product contribution, enabling data-driven business decisions.

This project demonstrates end-to-end Business Intelligence development, including data transformation, data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive visual reporting.

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data across stores, products, and promotional campaigns. However, identifying key performance drivers and understanding the effectiveness of promotions can be challenging without a centralized reporting solution.

Business stakeholders often need answers to questions such as:

Which stores are generating the highest sales?
How effective are promotional campaigns?
Which product categories contribute most to revenue?
What products require strategic attention?
How do sales vary across stores and categories?
## Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to:

Monitor overall retail performance.
Evaluate promotion effectiveness.
Analyze product and category contribution.
Identify sales trends and business opportunities.
Enable faster decision-making through interactive visualizations.
## Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following technologies:

 Power BI Desktop – Data visualization and dashboard development.<br>
 Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.<br>
 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures, KPIs, and business calculations.<br>
 Data Modeling – Relationship management between retail datasets.<br>
 PBIX File Format – Power BI development file.<br>
 Dashboard Pages<br>
## 1️ Store Performance Analysis<br>

Provides a comprehensive view of store-level performance.

Key Insights:
Store-wise sales comparison
Revenue performance tracking
Top-performing stores identification
Store contribution analysis
Performance benchmarking
## 2️ Promotion Type Analysis

Evaluates the impact of different promotional activities on sales.

Key Insights:
Promotion-wise sales performance
Promotional effectiveness analysis
Revenue generated by each campaign type
Comparison of promotional strategies
Identification of high-performing promotions
## 3️ Product & Category Analysis

Analyzes product-level and category-level business performance.

Key Insights:
Best-selling products
Category contribution to overall sales
Product performance trends
Revenue distribution across categories
Identification of growth opportunities
